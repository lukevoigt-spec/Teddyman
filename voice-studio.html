HERO TEDDY · VOICE STUDIO

This tool creates voicepack.js — the studio voices for the game. Two ways to fill a line: Generate with ElevenLabs, or Upload your own recording (voice memo .m4a/.mp3 works). Tip: the six letter sounds (snd_s … snd_n) are the most important lines in the whole game — recording those yourself in a quiet room gives the most accurate phonics. When done, tap Download, then upload index.html + voicepack.js together (as a zip) to your host.
Studio loaded and ready. If tapping buttons does nothing, this file is being previewed without JavaScript — upload it briefly to Netlify Drop and open the link instead.
